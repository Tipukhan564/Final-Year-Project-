What is Parkinson's Disease?

Parkinson's disease is a progressive nervous system disorder that affects movement. Learn more about the symptoms, diagnosis, and treatments available to help manage this condition.

Symptoms

Parkinson's symptoms include tremors, stiffness, and balance problems. Early signs may be subtle and progress over time.

Diagnosis

Diagnosing Parkinson's involves a combination of medical history, physical examinations, and specialized tests like DaTscan.

Treatment Options

While there is no cure, treatment options like medication, therapy, and surgery can help manage symptoms.
